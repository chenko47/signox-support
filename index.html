Frequently Asked Questions

My device doesn't appear during BLE scanning.
Make sure the device is in BLE mode (hold the button for 6 seconds until the blue light appears), that Bluetooth is enabled on your phone, and that you are within 10 metres of the device.

I can't log in — "Invalid credentials" error.
Double-check your Operator ID and Access Code. Both are case-sensitive. If you've forgotten your credentials, tap "Forgot password" on the login screen or contact your organization admin.

The app asks for Bluetooth permission but I accidentally denied it.
Go to Settings → SignOx → Bluetooth on your iPhone/iPad and enable the toggle.

My device shows as "not registered to you".
The device may already be claimed by another operator. Contact your admin to release it, or use the in-app "Register Device" flow to re-claim it.

How do I export diagnostic logs?
Go to Settings → Export Logs. A log file will be sent to your registered email address.
